# Title Options

Three options for the lecture title and one-liner. The title appears on the opening slide, in the syllabus, and anywhere the talk is promoted. The one-liner goes beneath it.

---

### Option A: The Conductor's Arc

**AI Agents: Conducting Your Own Orchestra**

_You're not going to manage AI. You're going to learn to conduct it — and the most important skill is knowing what your music sounds like._

---

### Option B: Direct and Practical

**AI Agents: From Architecture to Your First Build**

_What agents actually are, how they work, and how to design and build your own — starting today._

---

### Option C: Psychology-Forward

**AI Agents Through a Cognitive Lens**

_Every component of an AI agent maps to a cognitive function you already study. That makes you the best people to design them._
